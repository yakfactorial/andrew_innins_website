Knots of Our Own Making is a comedic interview-style podcast that was started in early 2015. Every other week, Andy, the co-host Heather, and various others interview an interesting person working in the fields of science, technology, or the arts, in order to find out how and why they do the things that they do. Previous guests have included researchers working in the University of Colorado Biology department and several high-profile acts in the Denver music scene.

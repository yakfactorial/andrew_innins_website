With the help and encouragement of friends and family, Andy got his start in music playing the guitar and other instruments over 10 years ago, and has been actively playing and producing music ever since. You can find his previous projects under different names, most prominently the AI Project, Controlisphere, and Disir. He started working under the name Aidisi in 2012 while living in Atlanta, and under this name has collaborated with musician friends across the country. Find Aidisi on Bandcamp, Spotify, iTunes, and wherever music is sold digitally.
